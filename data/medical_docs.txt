Medical Knowledge Base

Dengue:
- Symptoms: high fever, headache, pain behind eyes, joint pain, rash.
- Treatment: hydration, rest, paracetamol for fever. Avoid NSAIDs like ibuprofen.

Diabetes:
- Symptoms: frequent urination, excessive thirst, fatigue.
- Management: insulin or oral medication, diet control, regular exercise.

Hypertension:
- Symptoms: often none, sometimes headache or dizziness.
- Management: lifestyle changes, low-salt diet, medication if prescribed.
