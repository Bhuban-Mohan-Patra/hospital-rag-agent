Hospital Policy Knowledge Base

Appointment Policy:
- Patients must book appointments at least 24 hours in advance when possible.
- Walk-ins are allowed depending on doctor availability.

Cancellation Policy:
- Appointments should be cancelled at least 12 hours before the scheduled time.

Billing Policy:
- Payments can be made via cash, card, or digital payment methods.
- Insurance claims must be submitted with valid documents.

Visitor Policy:
- Only one attendant allowed per patient in ICU.
